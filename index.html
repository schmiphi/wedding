Information

Some information about the wedding

Getting There

The ceremony and reception will both take place at Pembroke Lodge, Richmond Park, Queen Street, Richmond, TW10 5HX.

Arrival Time

Day guests: Please arrive at 12:30pm and follow the signs directing you to The Russell Suite upon arrival.

Evening guests: Please arrive from 6.15pm and follow the signs directing you to The Russell Suite upon arrival.

Car parking

Guests who are driving should leave extra time to get parked as the car park is open to the general public and can therefore get extremely busy. Cars can be left overnight provided a parking permit is displayed in your window screen. These are freely available at the venue.
