Information

Getting There

The ceremony and reception will both take place at Pembroke Lodge, Richmond Park, Queen Street, Richmond, TW10 5HX.

Arrival Time

Day guests: Please arrive at 12:30pm and follow the signs directing you to The Russell Suite upon arrival.

Evening guests: Please arrive from 6.15pm and follow the signs directing you to The Russell Suite upon arrival.

Car Parking

Guests who are driving should leave extra time to get parked as the car park is open to the general public and can therefore get extremely busy. Cars can be left overnight provided a parking permit is displayed in your window screen. These are freely available at the venue.

Please note that the only access and exit points after dusk will be at Richmond Gate on the Star and Garter roundabout where a gate man will be stationed.

Taxis

Please pre-book your carriages to arrive at 11pm. Recommended taxi companies:

Parker Cars: 0208 560 0000

Riverside Cars: 020 8878 7777

Rugby Cars: 0208 891 5000

Accommodation

There are plenty of charming hotels in the Richmond Hill area. Those closest to Pembroke Lodge include:

The Petersham

The Bingham

Richmond Hill Hotel

Richmond also has a Premier Inn and a Travelodge near the town centre. These are a short taxi ride away from the venue.

Children

Unfortunately we are unable to accommodate children due to number restrictions. Please make alternative arrangements and, parents, enjoy your day off!

Dietary Requirements

Should you have any special dietary requirements, please specify this when responding to us so we can notify the catering team.
